## About Me

I am an Information Technology undergraduate at **NIT Jalandhar** (graduating 2026), working at the intersection of **AI/ML engineering**, **NLP research**, **time-series forecasting**, and **open-source systems**. I build practical ML systems that solve real-world problems, from renewable energy optimization to multimodal hate speech detection.

---

## Highlights

- **AI/ML Engineer Intern at Greenko Group** — built wind power generation scheduling models for 30+ renewable energy plants using time-series forecasting at 15-minute intervals
- **Renewable Energy Forecasting** — reduced scheduling MAE from **7.28 MWh to 3.70 MWh** using Ridge + LightGBM ensemble; deployed Streamlit dashboard and a local Ollama-based RAG chatbot for operators
- **NLP/Fairness Research** — developed COUNTER-HATE, a multimodal framework for bias-aware hate speech detection using counterfactual augmentation, LLMs, and adversarial training
- **Open-Source Contributor** — built CryptoStats, a KDE Plasma 6 cryptocurrency pricing widget using QML and curl for the Linux desktop ecosystem
- **Interested in** production ML systems, RAG pipelines, LLMs, and multimodal AI
- **Targeting** AI/ML Engineer, NLP Engineer, and ML Research Engineer roles
